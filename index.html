About Feltoks

Meet Our CEO

Omotoke Olugbenro, the brain behind this establishment found her love for fashion designing when she was just a kid. She used to stitch pieces of cloths together with a needle and thread to form wearable but unpresentable dresses and since then, she couldn't stop.

She pursued her dreams by registering for an apprenticeship program where she learned the basics of fashion designing. The program along with thousands of hours of practice made her what she is now.

She is currently studying educational management at TASUED
